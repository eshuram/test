Indira Gandhi

Prime Minister of India (1966–1977, 1980–1984)

Born: 19 Nov 1917 Died: 31 Oct 1984 Party: Indian National Congress

About

Indira Gandhi was the first woman Prime Minister of India. Known for her strong leadership, she guided India through significant challenges like the 1971 war and the Green Revolution.
